Calculate temperature in Celsius biasing on known base dist/temp

c = sqrt(X*R*T), where: ñ - speed of sound, m/s X - adiabatic index (is about 1.4 for air under normal conditions of pressure and temperature) R - gas constant (for air approximately 8.3145 J/mol·K, ~286.9 J/kg·K) T - the absolute temperature in kelvin

uint16_t Ultrasonic::us2cm ( unsigned long  microseconds)

Convert ping duration to centimeters

The speed of sound is 340 m/s or 29 microseconds per centimeter. The ping travels out and back, so to find the distance to the obstacle we take half of the distance traveled.
